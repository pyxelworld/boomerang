Boomerang

A secure internet


Secure

Boomerang is a secure browser powered by Chromium, the browser engine for Google Chrome and controlled by Pyxel.

Boomerang offers a secure search powered by Google called Boomerang Search (previously PyxelNET). Find websites, photos and news in the best and simplier way possible.


Easy

Boomerang is easy to use with the same design as Chrome and the touch of Pyxel. Boomerang offers the best user-friendly interface possible.


The touch of Pyxel

Enjoy the safety of Boomerang Search and the power of Pyxel's World in your own browser.



All this is in one single app. Boomerang browser.
